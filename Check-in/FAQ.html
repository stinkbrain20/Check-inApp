  • contactsValid Photo ID
    For everyone's security, our airports team has to verify your valid photo ID matches with the name on your itinerary. For senior citizens and persons with disability, the OSCA ID and PWD ID must be presented upon check-in.
  • card_travelPrepaid Luggage
    Bags for check-in must not exceed 32 kilos per piece, and not contain fragile or valuable items. You may carry one item inside the aircraft cabin, prrovided it weighs 7 kilos or less.
  • invert_colorsLiquids
    Subject to the prevalent applicable local laws and regulations guest may take liquids on board in their cabin baggae with a maximum volume of 100ml. The items must be placed in a transparent, re-sealable plastic bag.
